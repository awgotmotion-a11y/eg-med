About Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)

Medication-Assisted Treatment combines FDA-approved medications like Suboxone (buprenorphine/naloxone) with compassionate clinical care to treat opioid use disorder. MAT is the most evidence-based approach available — reducing withdrawal symptoms, cravings, and the risk of overdose. Dr. Wolfington provides non-judgmental evaluations and ongoing MAT management. Walk-ins are accepted for initial consultations.

What to expect: Dr. Wolfington will review your history, discuss your goals, and determine an appropriate starting plan. Follow-up appointments monitor progress and adjust care as needed. There is no shame here — this is healthcare.

  • New patient opioid use disorder evaluation
  • Suboxone induction & dosing management
  • Ongoing MAT follow-up visits
  • Private, judgment-free consultation

About Medical Marijuana Certification in Florida

Florida's Medical Marijuana Use Registry allows qualified patients to legally purchase and use cannabis products from any licensed dispensary in the state. Dr. Wolfington is a licensed Florida certifying physician. Your certification is entered into the state registry same day or next business day, and you can then apply for your official MMUR ID card online through the Florida Department of Health.

Common qualifying conditions include:

  • Chronic pain
  • PTSD
  • Cancer
  • Epilepsy & seizure disorders
  • Glaucoma
  • HIV / AIDS
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Crohn's disease / IBD
  • ALS / Parkinson's disease
  • Terminal conditions
  • Anxiety & depression (as determined by physician)
  • Other debilitating conditions
